Severus Snape

The Half Blood Prince

Life Summary:

  • 1/9/1960: Severus Snape is born
  • 9/1/1971: Snape sorted into Slytherin
  • 1976: Snape develops a crush on Lily and a rivalry with her boyfriend James
  • ~1979/80: Snape overhears partial prophecy fortelling downfall of his master, Lord Voldemort, and immediately rushes to warn him
  • August 1980: Snape arranges to meet with Dumbledore to warn him about attack on the Potters after discovering his old crush, Lily, was in danger
  • ~1981: Snape is hired as Potions Master after being denied his desired position as instructor of Defense Against the Dark Arts
  • 9/6/1991: Professor Snape begins emotionally abusing the son of his former rival because he is a spitting image of him except for the eyes. This goes on for 7 years
  • ~1992: Following the orders of Dumbledore, the only one Voldemort feared, Snape helps prevent Quirrel from bringing Voldemort back
  • 1/13/1996: After years of mental abuse, Snape is allowed to begin using magic to mentally rape Harry Potter in the guise of helping him defend himself against Voldemorts mindrape
  • 7/1996: Severus Snape makes Unbreakable Vow to kill Albus Dumbledore
  • 6/30/1997: Snape kills Dumbledore
  • 7/27/1997: Snape aids Voldemort and the Death Eaters on their attack on Harry Potter during his transport from his protected muggle home
  • 8/1997: Snape takes over as Hogwarts Headmaster
  • 12/26/1997: Snape uses his patronus to help Harry find a horcrux after the guilt of everything he's done has caught up with him
  • 5/1/1998: Voldemort has Snape killed, Snape tries to manipulate history by curating some memories to trick Harry into thinking that he did it all for love
